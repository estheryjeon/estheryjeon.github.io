Read Receipts
–
Critical Design

Using my personal Instagram data, I printed receipts with a receipt printer and thermal paper. I printed conversations on Instagram direct message, grouping them by user and their unique QR code. This projects shows the physical amount of messages that are stored on the cloud while also questioning the hoarding aspect that 'unlimited storage' brings to life.
