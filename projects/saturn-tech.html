Saturn Tech
–
Visual Identity

Saturn Tech is a start-up built by students, for students by. The company strives to solve high schoolers' unmitigated nightmare called block scheduling while building a better student experience with real-time updates on their school and fast communication with school members. Saturn is the block schedule hero. The logo looks back at Roman mythology's god of time, Saturn, reflecting the wonder and value of time and the universe with bold colors.
