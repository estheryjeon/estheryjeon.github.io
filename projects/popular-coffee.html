Popular Coffee
–
Programming, Data Visualization

For the past 4 years, I have fallen in love with the Philadelphia coffee shops (and in turn, it totally fed my addiction to coffee). When the project prompt was to visualize data, I immediately thought about how I could make it easier to choose coffee shops in a busy city like Philadelphia. The answer: Google's popular time data. Since the API is unavailable, I used a populartimes library to get the json data and then fed it to my map of Philly to indicate how busy a coffee shop is through opacity. The dreaded question of "Will there be a seat at that coffee shop?" is over.
