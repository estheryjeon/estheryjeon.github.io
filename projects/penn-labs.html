Penn Labs
–
Visual Identity

Penn Labs is a team of student software engineers, product designers, and business developers. We create products for the Penn community with an emphasis on education and resources. In my last year, I wanted to re-brand our visual identity to define who we are and create a resource for future designers and developers. The guide was created with the Labs design team; I was in charge of the logo, colors, and typography. As Labs is ever-evolving, this isn't final and I'm so excited to see how it grows!
