How Are You?

An experimental project to explore the interaction and response of the default question "how are you?" through typography, form, and layout. Words and phrases were chosen from a Google form sent out to peers during finals week. Printed on cardstock and trimmed to 5" by 10" spreads.

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign
