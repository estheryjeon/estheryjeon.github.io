How Are You?
–
Book Design, Typography

When I asked this question to my friends, I noticed how the default answer was always "good". Why was this so? And was there a way to elicit a more sincere response? I decided to explore if and how responding anonymously would impact the results. This never-ending accordion-bound book studies the interaction and response to the default question how are you?
