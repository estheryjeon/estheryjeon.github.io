I came to the realization that I’m not a very outwardly expressive person, but I found that I often express personal feelings visually (mainly through Instagram these days). I love the concept of embedding personal ideas in a visual form that lets you abstract those ideas and let the world respond. So, I took it on myself to start this project where I express my thoughts through design.

The word design is very vague, yes. This could take form through print design, lettering, animation, and more. This is a space for me to explore the world of design and my push my limits and understanding of intentional design. So, here’s to new things!
