Have You Seen This Person?
–
Google Maps Street View
Google Maps Street View is a photographic archive created by a car constantly taking photos in efforts to capture the world digitally. This project explores concerns of this massive digital archive of images. What is considered invasion of privacy if Google can roam around the world taking photos without consent? These unidentifiable people are just left to the vulnerability of the digital world.
