Vital Circle

COVID-19 has thrown our lives upside down, causing uncertainty and fear. During this time, I wanted to find a way to help others through my skills. And this was what came my way: volunteering on a COVID-19 symptom tracking app called Vital Circle. The goal was to help employers and schools track symptoms across their organization. The timeline for this project was really short and I ended up having to leave the team due to time commitment issues. But in that short time, I was able to design all the MVP flows: sign-up, daily symptom logging, record of check-ins, and COVID-19 resources. It was a lot. But it showed me how much I've grown my design instincts this past year and made me even more excited to see how I develop my skills!
