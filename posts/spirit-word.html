Spirit Word

I'm about 10 years late to the Naruto scene, but in my senior year of college, I finally watched all of Naruto. And I must say it was the most epic show I have ever seen – loyal friendship, sick battles, and iconic characters. I'm unashamed to say that I'm a total geek about Naruto now, so much so, that I created fan zines about the main characters: Naruto, Sasuke, and Sakura. This is an ode to Naruto, the greatest show of all time.
